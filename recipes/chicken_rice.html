Chicken Rise

Tell me about it

This simple and delicious one-pot chicken and rice dish is a must-try for your family, and I am sure it will become a staple weeknight meal.
It is my favorite weeknight meal to prepare because it is super quick and well-balanced!

Ingredients

  • 2 Small chicken breasts
  • 1 Cup of rice
  • 1 Medium onion
  • 2 Carrots
  • 3-5 Cups of water
  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • Seasoning to taste

How to make it:

  1. Start by preparing all your ingredients; this helps speed up the cooking process.
    Wash, peel, and grate carrots and set aside. Do the same with the onion.
    Prepare the rice by rinsing one cup in a colander; this gets some of the starch.
  2. Add your diced onion with one tablespoon of oil and sauté on medium for a few minutes in a Dutch oven or pot.
  3. Add raw chicken chunks and cook until browned about 5-6 minutes. Add the second tablespoon of oil if needed.
  4. Add grated carrots, stir, and after a few minutes, add rinsed rice.
  5. Mix everything well and add 3-4 cups of water. Your rice should be covered in water – don’t overfill it; it will turn into soup.
  6. Simmer on low for 15-20 minutes, checking in and mixing every five or so minutes.
  7. Use a fork to fluff rice and serve.
